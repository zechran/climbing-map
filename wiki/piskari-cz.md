# piskari.cz

**Summary**: Community web portal for traditional Czech sandstone climbing in the Polická pánev region, covering five climbing areas.

**Sources**: piskari.cz (direct observation)

**Last updated**: 2026-05-25

---

piskari.cz (https://www.piskari.cz) is the central online resource for climbers active in the [[policka-panev]] (Police Basin). It was co-financed by ERDF funds via Euroregion Glacensis as a cross-border project promoting traditional sandstone climbing in the Polická pánev and the Polish Góry Stołowe (Stolové hory).

## What the portal covers

- Route database with descriptions, grades, and user comments for all five areas ([[adrspach]], [[broumovske-steny]], [[ostas]], [[teplicke-skaly]], [[krizovy-vrch]])
- News and updates from [[ovk-broumovsko]] (meeting minutes, route securing, nesting closures)
- Photo gallery from current climbing season
- Articles and trip reports (e.g. Pražský trad, Tatras trips)
- Community comments on individual routes — the primary source for real-time route conditions, gear beta, and difficulty opinions

## Route URL structure

Individual routes are accessible at:
`https://www.piskari.cz/cs/cesta/[route-slug]-[ID]/`

Each route has a numeric ID (e.g. Gotes = 8251, Ženšen = 9). Comments are linked via `#komentarXXXXX`.
