# Žďárská vyhlídka

**Summary**: A sandstone tower/viewpoint on Ostaš with the route Jarničky, which was re-secured in 2025.

**Sources**: ovkbroumovsko.cz (meeting minutes Aug 2025)

**Last updated**: 2026-05-25

---

Žďárská vyhlídka (Žďár viewpoint) is a sandstone formation on [[ostas]], functioning as both a climbing tower and a named viewpoint. The route [[routes/jarnicka]] on this formation was re-secured and was an agenda item at the [[ovk-broumovsko]] meeting of August 2025.

## Location

- **Area**: [[ostas]]
- **Sub-area**: Not specified (Horní / Zadní Ostaš TBD)
