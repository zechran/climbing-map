# Broumovské stěny (Broumovky)

**Summary**: One of the five Czech sandstone climbing areas in the Polická pánev, known as "Broumovky".

**Sources**: piskari.cz

**Last updated**: 2026-05-25

---

Broumovské stěny (colloquially "Broumovky") is one of the five climbing areas in the [[policka-panev]], governed by [[ovk-broumovsko]].

This area is mentioned in the portal overview but no routes, towers, or specific details were present in the initial source. The area is part of the broader Broumovsko protected landscape (CHKO Broumovsko).
