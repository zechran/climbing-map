# Dolní Adršpach (Lower Adršpach)

**Summary**: Sub-area of Adršpach covering the lower (southern) part of the rock town, containing multiple named sectors with free-standing sandstone towers.

**Sources**: piskari.cz

**Last updated**: 2026-05-25

---

Dolní Adršpach (Lower Adršpach) is a geographic sub-area within [[adrspach]]. It groups the sectors situated in the lower / southern part of the Adršpašské skály rock town. Towers are scraped from piskari.cz with GPS extracted from the JavaScript map on each tower page.
