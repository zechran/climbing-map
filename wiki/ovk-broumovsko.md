# OVK Broumovsko

**Summary**: Oblastní vrcholová komise Broumovsko — the regional climbing committee that governs all climbing activity across the five areas of the Polická pánev.

**Sources**: ovkbroumovsko.cz (meeting minutes 2025–2026)

**Last updated**: 2026-05-25

---

OVK Broumovsko (Oblastní vrcholová komise Broumovsko) is the body responsible for governing [[czech-sandstone-climbing]] in the [[policka-panev]]. It operates under the Czech Mountaineering Association (ČHS — Český horolezecký svaz) and coordinates with the regional protected landscape authority (OP). Website: https://ovkbroumovsko.cz

## Responsibilities

- Setting and enforcing rules for first ascents (prvovýstupy), including debates over gear style (e.g. saské stavění — Saxon-style placing)
- Managing route maintenance and re-securing of old routes
- Coordinating seasonal nature closures (nesting birds, protected species)
- Installing information boards for the climbing public
- Archiving summit books (vrcholové knížky) from rock formations in the area
- Liaising with national parks / landscape protection authorities
