Věž Rybník (Rybník Tower)

Summary: A named sandstone tower on Horní Ostaš with the route Doutníky, which was being re-secured by OVK Broumovsko as of May 2026.

Sources: ovkbroumovsko.cz (meeting minutes May 2026)

Last updated: 2026-05-25

Věž Rybník (Rybník tower) is a sandstone free-standing tower in Ostaš, specifically on Horní Ostaš (Upper Ostaš). The tower is the subject of active route maintenance coordinated by OVK Broumovsko.

Location

  • Area: Ostaš
  • Sub-area: Horní Ostaš

Routes

Route Grade Notes
Doutníky Unknown Being secured/dojištěno as of May 2026

Governance note

The securing of Doutníky (“dojištění cesty Doutníky na Rybník na Horním Ostaši”) was an agenda item at the OVK Broumovsko meeting of 8 May 2026.
