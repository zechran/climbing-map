Žďárská vyhlídka

Summary: A sandstone tower/viewpoint on Ostaš with the route Jarničky, which was re-secured in 2025.

Sources: ovkbroumovsko.cz (meeting minutes Aug 2025)

Last updated: 2026-05-25

Žďárská vyhlídka (Žďár viewpoint) is a sandstone formation on Ostaš, functioning as both a climbing tower and a named viewpoint. The route Routes/Jarnicka on this formation was re-secured and was an agenda item at the OVK Broumovsko meeting of August 2025.
