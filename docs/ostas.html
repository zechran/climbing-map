Ostaš

Summary: Sandstone climbing area in the Polická pánev, divided into Horní Ostaš (Upper) and Zadní Ostaš (Rear), with some of the area’s most prized and demanding routes.

Sources: piskari.cz, ovkbroumovsko.cz

Last updated: 2026-05-25

Ostaš is one of the five climbing areas in the Polická pánev (Police Basin). It is split into two administratively distinct sub-areas: Horní Ostaš (Upper Ostaš) and Zadní Ostaš (Rear Ostaš). The area contains some highly regarded routes and is also subject to active governance discussions.

Sub-areas

Horní Ostaš (Upper Ostaš)

  • Contains Věž Rybník (Rybník Tower) (Rybník tower) with route Doutníky (being secured as of May 2026)
  • Exception request filed with OVK Broumovsko for continued climbing here

Zadní Ostaš (Rear Ostaš)

  • Under evaluation for a first ascent ban (uzávěra pro prvovýstupy); actively discussed at OVK meetings in Jan and May 2026
  • Exception request filed for climbing here

Known towers

  • Věž Rybník (Rybník Tower) — Rybník tower, Horní Ostaš
  • Žďárská vyhlídka — Žďárská vyhlídka (viewpoint), location on Ostaš
  • Jirkovy věže — near Frýdlantská vyhlídka; in nesting closure zone (2026)
  • Stěna Mladých pánů — starting boundary of the nesting closure (2026)

Active closures (2026)

Peregrine falcon nesting near Frýdlantská vyhlídka — no climbing in the section from Stěna Mladých pánů to Jirkovy věže (announced 2026-03-13 by OVK Broumovsko).

Notable routes

  • Nenázvuhodná prasečina — one of the top-rated routes on Ostaš; IXc/Xa
  • Doutníky — on Věž Rybník (Rybník Tower); being re-secured May 2026
  • Jarničky — on Žďárská vyhlídka; re-secured 2025
