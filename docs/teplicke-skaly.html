Teplické skály (Teplice)

Summary: Sandstone climbing area in the Polická pánev, adjacent to Adršpach, together forming the Adršpašsko-teplické skály nature reserve.

Sources: piskari.cz, ovkbroumovsko.cz

Last updated: 2026-05-25

Teplické skály (colloquially “Teplice”) is one of the five climbing areas in the Polická pánev (Police Basin), immediately neighbouring Adršpach (Ádr). Together they form the Adršpašsko-teplické skály national nature reserve (NPP).

Known sectors / features

  • Vstupní rokle — entrance gorge. A new custodian (správce) was appointed in 2025 (announced at OVK Broumovsko meeting Aug 2025).

Access & governance

Managed by OVK Broumovsko. Climbing information boards covering “Pravidla lezení v Adršpašsko-Teplických skalách” (climbing rules in the Adršpašsko-Teplické skály) were being developed as of 2025.
